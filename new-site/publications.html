Publications

Digital Collateral
The Quarterly Journal of Economics, 2024, 139(3): 1713–1766
with Paul Gertler and Catherine Wolfram
We study a new form of lending in developing countries that uses digital collateral—the threat of remotely disabling a device—to secure loans for smartphones and other consumer durables. Using "lockout" technology, lenders can enforce repayment even absent traditional collateral or credit histories.
Paper

with Dong Wei
